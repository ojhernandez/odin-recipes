Buttered Toast

Description

This buttered toast is a great addition to your morning breakfast! It is also super easy to make and doesn't require a lot of ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Adjust toaster to desired doneness setting.
  2. Place bread in toaster.
  3. Toast Bread
  4. After bread pops up, spread butter on toast while it is warm.
